Flower (2025)

A 16mm film experiment exploring image-making beyond controlled processes. Instead of standard development, the film was processed using unconventional substances such as vitamin C and tea, allowing chemical reactions to reshape the footage. Colors bleed, shift, and destabilize; textures emerge unpredictably; parts of the image dissolve or intensify. Rather than documenting reality, the work reveals film as a material in flux—where chance, time, and chemistry actively participate in the creation of the image.
